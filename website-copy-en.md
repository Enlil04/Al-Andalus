### Cargo Insurance (Marine / Land / Air)

**H2: Cargo Insurance**

Goods in transit face a different set of risks at every stage — loading, transport, customs, delivery. Cargo insurance covers loss or damage to goods moving by sea, road, or air, giving exporters, importers, distributors, and logistics operators a defined financial backstop when something goes wrong in the supply chain.

**Who it's for:** Trading companies, freight forwarders, manufacturers, and any business that ships goods domestically or internationally.

---

### Engineering Insurance (Contractors' All Risks)

**H2: Engineering Insurance — Contractors' All Risks**

Construction projects involve heavy equipment, multiple contractors, and timelines that do not pause for accidents. Contractors' All Risks insurance covers construction projects, machinery, and equipment against accidental damage and unforeseen events during execution — so a single incident does not derail an entire build.

**Who it's for:** Contractors, project owners, engineering firms, and infrastructure developers.

---

### Glass Insurance

**H2: Glass Insurance**

Glass facades, storefront panels, and display windows are expensive to replace and vulnerable to breakage. This policy provides compensation for damage or breakage to glass panels, facades, and related installations — covering repair or replacement costs without absorbing the full hit on your balance sheet.

**Who it's for:** Retailers, office buildings, showrooms, and commercial properties with significant glass installations.

---

### Burglary Insurance

**H2: Burglary Insurance**

Theft and unlawful entry can wipe out inventory, equipment, or cash reserves in a single night. Burglary insurance protects property and assets against losses from theft, burglary, or break-in — giving business owners a defined recovery path after a criminal loss.

**Who it's for:** Shops, warehouses, offices, and any commercial premises holding stock or valuables.

---

### Cash in Transit and Safe Insurance

**H2: Cash in Transit and Safe Insurance**

Cash is at its most vulnerable when it moves. This policy covers cash during transport or while held in approved safes against theft, loss, and other covered perils — a standard requirement for businesses, retailers, and institutions that handle physical currency regularly.

**Who it's for:** Banks, exchange houses, retailers, and organizations that move or store cash on-site.

---

### Public Liability Insurance

**H2: Public Liability Insurance**

If your operations cause bodily injury or property damage to a third party, the financial consequences can be significant. Public liability insurance protects the insured against legal liabilities arising from such incidents — covering defense costs and compensation where the policy applies.

**Who it's for:** Businesses with public premises, contractors, event organizers, and service providers interacting with the public.

---

### Fire Insurance

**H2: Fire Insurance**

Fire remains one of the most destructive risks for commercial and residential property. Fire insurance covers losses and damages resulting from fire and related perils — including, for commercial shops, fires caused by electrical faults, accidental negligence, or spread from neighboring premises covered under the policy.

**Who it's for:** Commercial shops, offices, warehouses, factories, and property owners.

*Burglary coverage can be added to a fire policy upon request, subject to an additional premium for the selected risk.*

---

### Fidelity Guarantee Insurance

**H2: Fidelity Guarantee Insurance**

Employee dishonesty, fraud, and breach of trust can cause losses that standard property policies do not address. Fidelity guarantee insurance protects companies against financial losses resulting from such acts by employees — a practical safeguard for any organization with staff handling money, inventory, or sensitive assets.

**Who it's for:** Companies with employees in financial, inventory, or administrative roles.

---

### Motor Insurance

**H2: Motor Insurance**

Vehicles are essential to daily life and business operations in Iraq — and accidents create costs that add up fast. Motor insurance provides comprehensive coverage for vehicles against accidents, physical damage, and legal liabilities arising from their use.

**Who it's for:** Individual vehicle owners, fleet operators, logistics companies, and any organization running company vehicles.

---

### Health Insurance

**H2: Health Insurance**

Medical costs for major procedures can be overwhelming without coverage. Health insurance provides comprehensive healthcare coverage for medical treatment, hospitalization, and related expenses — including major surgical procedures and laparoscopic surgeries performed under general anesthesia.

Covered expenses typically include hospitalization, surgeons' and physicians' fees, companion accommodation where applicable, examinations, medications, laboratory tests, and diagnostic imaging.

**Who it's for:** Individuals, families, and employers seeking group or individual health coverage.

---

### Travel Insurance

**H2: Travel Insurance**

Travel involves risks you cannot fully control — medical emergencies abroad, lost baggage, accidents. Travel insurance covers travel-related risks including accidents, medical emergencies, and loss of baggage, so a disrupted trip does not become a financial crisis.

**Who it's for:** Individual travelers, business travelers, and organizations sending staff abroad.

---

### Medical Malpractice Insurance

**H2: Medical Malpractice Insurance**

Healthcare professionals and institutions face professional liability exposure every working day. Medical malpractice insurance protects practitioners and medical facilities against claims arising from professional negligence or errors — covering the legal and financial consequences of covered incidents.

**Who it's for:** Doctors, surgeons, clinics, hospitals, and other licensed medical practitioners and institutions.

---

### Hull Insurance

**H2: Hull Insurance**

Vessels and their equipment represent significant capital at sea. Hull insurance covers physical loss or damage to vessels and associated marine equipment — protecting ship owners and operators against the financial impact of maritime incidents.

**Who it's for:** Ship owners, maritime operators, and businesses with vessels in commercial use.

## FAQ

### H1: Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the purpose of group and individual health insurance?**

Group and individual health insurance is designed to cover major surgical procedures, including laparoscopic surgeries performed under general anesthesia. The policy covers eligible medical expenses during treatment: hospitalization costs, surgeons' and physicians' fees, companion accommodation where applicable, medical examinations, medications, laboratory tests, and diagnostic imaging services.

**What does fire and burglary insurance for commercial shops cover?**

This policy covers losses and damages from fire caused by electrical short circuits, accidental negligence, or fires originating in neighboring premises that spread to the insured property. Burglary and theft coverage can be added upon the insured's request, subject to payment of the applicable additional premium for the selected risks.

**What does individual and group personal accident insurance cover?**

Personal accident insurance covers accidental events that result in bodily injury, permanent disability, or accidental death. Coverage applies only to accidents — it does not extend to death or disability from natural causes, illness, or pre-existing medical conditions.
