Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
1. What is the purpose of Group and Individual Health Insurance?
Group and Individual Health Insurance is designed to cover major surgical procedures, including laparoscopic surgeries performed under general anesthesia. This policy covers all eligible medical expenses incurred during treatment, including hospitalization costs, surgeons’ and physicians’ fees, accommodation for a companion when applicable, medical examinations, medications, laboratory tests, and diagnostic imaging services.
2. What does Fire and Burglary Insurance for Commercial Shops cover?
This insurance policy covers losses and damages resulting from fire caused by electrical short circuits, accidental negligence, or fires originating from neighboring premises that spread to the insured property. The policy can also be extended to include burglary and theft coverage, subject to the insured's request and payment of the applicable additional premium for the selected risks.


3. What does Individual and Group Personal Accident Insurance cover?
Personal Accident Insurance provides coverage against accidental events that result in bodily injury, permanent disability, or accidental death. Coverage applies only to accidents and does not extend to death or disability resulting from natural causes, illness, or pre-existing medical conditions.
